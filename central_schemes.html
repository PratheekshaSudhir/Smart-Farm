1 Kisan Credit Card Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) scheme was introduced in 1998 for issue of Kisan Credit Cards to farmers on the basis of their holdings for uniform adoption by the banks so that farmers may use them to readily purchase agriculture inputs and draw cash for their production needs. 2 Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare A contributory pension scheme by the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare for Individual Farmers. Provides an assured monthly pension of Rs. 3000/- to all the Small and Marginal farmers after the age of 60 years. Click Here
3 Krishi Unnati Yojana - MOVCDNER Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Realizing the potential of organic farming in the North Eastern Region of the country Ministry of Agriculture and Farmer Welfare has launched a Central Sector Scheme entitled “Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region” (MOVCDNER). Click Here
4 Kera Suraksha Insurance Scheme Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare The ‘Kera Suraksha Insurance Scheme’ is a comprehensive Group Personal Accident Insurance Scheme, implemented by Coconut Development Board in association with Public Insurance companies for Coconut Tree Climbers/Neera Technicians/Coconut Harvesters. Click Here
5 Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Farmer welfare scheme by Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare to provide income support to all landholding farmers' families in the country to supplement their financial needs for procuring various inputs related to agriculture and allied activities as well as domestic needs. Click Here
6 Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojna Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana was launched in 2016 with the aim to support farmers by providing an affordable comprehensive risk cover for crops. Click Here
7 National Agriculture Market Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare e-NAM a pan-India electronic trading portal was launched on 14th April 2016, by the Prime Minister of India, with the aim of networking the existing mandis on a common online market platform as “One Nation One Market” for agricultural commodities in India. Click Here
8 Agroforestry component under RKVY Ministry Of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Sub-Mission on Agroforestry was launched in 2016-17 to promote plantation on farmlands along with agricultural crops. The scheme aims to increase farm income, expand tree cover and promote better carbon sequestration for climate change adaptation and mitigation. Click Here
9 Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana Ministry of Fisheries,Animal Husbandry and Dairying Introduced by the Department of Fisheries, Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) is a scheme to bring about ecologically healthy, economically viable, and socially inclusive development of the fisheries sector of India. Click Here
11 National Scheme Of Welfare Of Fishermen Ministry of Fisheries,Animal Husbandry and Dairying The National Scheme of Welfare of Fishermen provides financial assistance to fishermen. They can use these for constructing houses and community halls for both recreation and work purposes. Further, through the amount availed under this scheme, fishermen can install tube wells.
